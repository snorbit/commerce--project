Don't Just Take the First Deal You See!

The mobile phone market is incredibly competitive, which is great news for you! It means there are always new deals and offers available. That's why it's so important to shop around and compare different plans before you commit. Here are a couple of examples of the types of deals you might find (as of late 2025):

Example 1: The Value-Packed Pre-Paid Plan

Dodo's $30 Pre-Paid Plan is a great example of a plan that offers a lot of value for a low price. For the first six months, it might be discounted, and it comes with:

  • No excess data charges: If you go over your data limit, your speed is just slowed down, so you won't get a huge bill.
  • Data banking: You can save your unused data for later.
  • International call credit: A set amount of credit for calling overseas.

This kind of plan is great if you're on a budget and want to avoid bill shock.

Example 2: The Student-Focused Contract Plan

Vodafone's Student Small SIM-only Plan is a good example of a plan designed specifically for students. It might require you to have a valid student ID, but it can offer some great perks, such as:

  • Bonus data: You might get a huge amount of extra data each month for the first year.
  • Unlimited international calls: To certain countries.
  • No excess data charges: Like the Dodo plan, your speed is just slowed down if you go over your limit.

This type of plan can be a good option if you use a lot of data and make international calls, and you're happy to commit to a contract.

The Bottom Line

These are just two examples, and deals are always changing. The best plan for you will depend on your individual needs and budget. That's why it's so important to use comparison websites and read the fine print before you sign up.
